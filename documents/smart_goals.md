## What are SMART Goals?

SMART is an acronym that ensures goals are:
- **S**pecific - Clear and well-defined
- **M**easurable - Quantifiable indicators of success
- **A**chievable - Realistic given resources and constraints
- **R**elevant - Aligned with broader career objectives
- **T**ime-bound - Has a clear deadline
