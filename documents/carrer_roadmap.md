# 5-Year Career Roadmap in Cybersecurity

## **Year 1: Building the Foundation**
- **Key Milestones**:
  - Develop foundational knowledge in cybersecurity, focusing on network security, ethical hacking, and risk management.
  - Complete **Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH)** certification.
  - Secure an internship in cybersecurity to gain hands-on experience.
  
- **Skills to Focus On**:
  - Networking fundamentals (TCP/IP, DNS, HTTP, etc.)
  - Basic penetration testing tools (Kali Linux, Metasploit)
  - Linux command-line proficiency

## **Year 2: Gaining Professional Experience**
- **Key Milestones**:
  - Secure a full-time position as a **Cybersecurity Analyst** or **Penetration Tester**.
  - Participate in cybersecurity competitions and hackathons to enhance practical skills.
  - Begin pursuing **Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP)** certification.

- **Skills to Focus On**:
  - Vulnerability assessment and penetration testing
  - Security policy creation and risk assessment
  - Incident response and forensic investigation
  
## **Year 3: Transitioning to Advanced Roles**
- **Key Milestones**:
  - Move into more advanced roles such as **Security Architect** or **Network Security Engineer**.
  - Complete CISSP certification and gain recognition in the cybersecurity field.
  - Build a strong online presence by contributing to cybersecurity forums and writing blogs.
  
- **Skills to Focus On**:
  - Security architecture and secure system design
  - Cloud security and advanced penetration testing techniques
  - Leadership and team management skills

## **Year 4: Leadership and Specialization**
- **Key Milestones**:
  - Lead a cybersecurity team, focusing on high-level risk management and security architecture.
  - Specialize in a niche area like cloud security, blockchain security, or IoT security.
  - Mentor junior cybersecurity professionals.

- **Skills to Focus On**:
  - Cloud security frameworks (AWS, Azure)
  - Advanced cryptography and secure communications
  - Security governance and compliance
  
## **Year 5: Becoming an Expert in Cybersecurity**
- **Key Milestones**:
  - Establish yourself as a subject matter expert in cybersecurity.
  - Contribute to the development of industry standards or certifications.
  - Take on strategic roles such as **Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)** or **Security Consultant**.
  
- **Skills to Focus On**:
  - Cutting-edge cybersecurity technologies like AI and machine learning for security
  - Developing security policies for global enterprises
  - Risk management at an organizational level
  
**Long-Term Goal**: To become a recognized leader in cybersecurity, responsible for designing and implementing enterprise-wide security strategies, and ensuring the protection of data and systems at the highest level.
